About me

Hello, I am a graduate student in Computer Science department at UMass, Amherst. I am in the database lab. My research area is in automatic interactive data exploration over large databases. Before that, I earned a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Science and Technology of China and a Master's degree in Mathematics and Statistics from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

Research

My research is in automatic interactive data exploration over large databases.
We have a demo paper and show our working system (AIDE: Automatic Interactive Data Exploration system) at VLDB 2015.
